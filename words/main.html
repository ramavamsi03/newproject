Word List

Below is a collection of interesting words and their meanings. Expand your vocabulary!

  1. Ephemeral (adjective): Lasting for a very short time.
    "The beauty of the sunset was ephemeral."
  2. Serendipity (noun): The occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.
    "Meeting her was pure serendipity."
  3. Quintessential (adjective): Representing the most perfect or typical example of a quality or class.
    "He was the quintessential gentleman."
  4. Luminescence (noun): The emission of light by a substance not resulting from heat.
    "The cave was filled with the luminescence of glowworms."
  5. Soliloquy (noun): An act of speaking one's thoughts aloud when by oneself.
    "The actor's soliloquy was mesmerizing."
  6. Petrichor (noun): The pleasant, earthy smell after rain.
    "She loved the petrichor that followed the storm."
  7. Labyrinthine (adjective): (of a network) like a labyrinth; irregular and twisting.
    "The labyrinthine alleys of the old city were fascinating."
  8. Obfuscate (verb): To render obscure, unclear, or unintelligible.
    "The details served only to obfuscate the real issue."
  9. Susurrus (noun): Whispering, murmuring, or rustling.
    "The susurrus of the trees was calming."
  10. Ebullient (adjective): Cheerful and full of energy.
    "Her ebullient personality lit up the room."
  11. Disparate (adjective): Essentially different in kind; not allowing comparison.
    "The two cultures were disparate."
  12. Halcyon (adjective): Denoting a period of time in the past that was idyllically happy and peaceful.
